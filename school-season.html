Boys School Season

General Information

Who can play

7th and 8th grade students currently enrolled at Lesher.

Team structure

36 total players across four teams:

  • Varsity
  • Junior Varsity
  • C Team
  • D Team

Tryouts

Students who do not make the final roster will receive a refund for athletic fees after tryouts.

What you need

  • Gym shoes
  • Workout clothes for practices
  • School uniforms (provided for competitions and returned at season's end)

Registration

All athletes must complete the registration process and pay the participation fee before tryouts.

Medical needs

Please notify coaches of any specific medical needs (e.g., asthma) before the season starts.

Open gym

Any student-athlete participating in open gym must have an updated physical form on file with the front office.
